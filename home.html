About Me

Marquos obtained his Bachelors with magna cum laude from King Fahd University majoring in computer engineering and a non-degree program from Entrepreneurship Institute at the same university. In 2018, Marquos moved to Canada to pursue his MSc degree at McMaster University in Hamilton, Ontario.

Marquos worked on several projects in the area of web development, software development and computer networking. His passion is to leverage his education in technology to advise companies during their digital transformation journey leveraging his particular skill at seeing the big picture and connecting the dots to achieve the overarching goals and ensure that they are well prepared for the upcoming 4th industrial revolution.
